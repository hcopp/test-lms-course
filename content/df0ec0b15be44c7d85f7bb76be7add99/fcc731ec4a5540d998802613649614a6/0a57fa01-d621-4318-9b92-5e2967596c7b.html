If your colleagues make comments or take action against you based on your race, ancestry, place of origin, political beliefs, religion, marital status, physical or mental disability, age, sex or sexual orientation, this is considered harassment. 

However, if your manager asks you about a project that didn't meet deadlines or the fact that you are always late for work, this does not constitute harassment, although you may not feel completely comfortable is such a situation.

Please move on to find out what harassment is and learn to identify and deal with harassment.
